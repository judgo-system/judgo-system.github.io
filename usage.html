Usage

Open your web browser and go to the URL [http://0.0.0.0:8000/)] of your judgo application.

Admin side

Enter your superuser credentials (username and password) that you set during the setup process (The default username and password is `admin`). Click the "Sign in" button. If the login is successful, you will be redirected to the main admin page, where you can manage the content and users of your judgo application.

As an administrator, you have full access to all topics, documents, and tasks. This allows you to manage the content and users. Here are some of the key functions you can perform in the JUDGO admin interface:

  • Topics management: You can create, edit, and delete topics, which are the main categories under which documents are organized in the judgo application.
  • Documents management: You can create, edit, and delete documents, which are the individual pieces of content that users rank in the judgo application.
  • Task management: You can create, edit, and delete tasks, which are the specific sets of documents that are assigned to users for ranking.
  • User management:You can create new users, edit existing users, and assign tasks to specific users.
  • User consistency tracking: You can monitor the consistency of users, which is a measure of how well they are ranking documents in accordance with the judgment algorithm.
  • Import/Export: You can import and export final results as a CSV file, which allows you to easily transfer the data to other applications or perform additional analysis.
With these capabilities, the judgo admin interface gives you complete control over the content, users, and results, so you can use it to support your research objectives.
